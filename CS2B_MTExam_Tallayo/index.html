Tourist Spots & Adventures

Pinsal Falls

Pinsal Falls is the largest waterfall in Ilocos Sur, located in the town of Sta. Maria. Its twin cascades create natural pools perfect for swimming and relaxation. Surrounded by lush greenery, it’s a top destination for nature lovers.

#PinsalFalls #Adventure #Nature #IlocosSur #Waterfalls

Calle Crisologo

Known for its old-world charm, shops, and horse-drawn carriages. Experience authentic Ilocano traditions. A must-see for every visitor.

#CalleCrisologo #Antiques #IlocosSur #Travel #Tradition

Cabugao Beach Cove

A white-sand paradise in the town of Cabugao, perfect for swimming and camping. The cove offers crystal-clear waters, stunning views, and relaxing vibes. It’s an ideal destination for family outings and beach lovers.

#CabugaoBeach #WhiteSand #BeachLife #IlocosSur #Travel

Ilocos Delicacies & Heritage Products

Ilocos Sur is famous for its flavorful delicacies and unique cultural products. From Vigan longganisa, empanada, and bagnet to sweet treats like tinubong, peanut brittle, and calamay, each item reflects Ilocano tradition. Iconic burnay jars and sukang Iloko also highlight the craftsmanship and culinary heritage of the province.
